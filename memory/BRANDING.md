# Fidelis Logic - Brand Guidelines

## Company Information
- **Legal Name:** Fidelis Logic LLC
- **Brand Name:** Fidelis Logic
- **Location:** Dubai, United Arab Emirates

## Logo Usage
### Files
- `/public/logo-color.png` - Full color logo (use on light backgrounds)
- `/public/logo-black.png` - Black logo (use on light backgrounds for print)
- `/public/logo-white.png` - White logo (use on dark backgrounds)

### Application
- **Header:** Color logo on white background
- **Footer:** White logo on dark background (#1F2937)
- **Minimum size:** 120px width for legibility
- **Clear space:** Minimum 20px padding around logo

## Brand Colors
### Primary Palette
- **Fidelis Cyan:** #22D3EE (rgb(34, 211, 238))
- **Fidelis Blue:** #2563EB (rgb(37, 99, 235))
- **Fidelis Dark Blue:** #1E40AF (rgb(30, 64, 175))

### Usage
- Primary CTA buttons: Fidelis Blue (#2563EB)
- Hover states: Fidelis Dark Blue (#1E40AF)
- Accents and highlights: Fidelis Cyan (#22D3EE)
- Gradient overlays: Cyan to Blue gradient

### Neutral Colors
- Text Primary: #111827 (gray-900)
- Text Secondary: #6B7280 (gray-500)
- Background: #FFFFFF (white)
- Card Background: #F9FAFB (gray-50)
- Footer Background: #1F2937 (gray-800)

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** System fonts (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto)
- **Headings:** Bold weight (600-700)
- **Body:** Regular weight (400)
- **Emphasis:** Medium weight (500)

## Design Philosophy
- **Style:** Apple-inspired minimalism
- **Whitespace:** High, generous spacing
- **Feel:** Premium, enterprise-grade, professional
- **Tone:** Confident, consultative, vendor-neutral

## Voice & Messaging
- **Positioning:** "Simplifying modern workplace technology decisions"
- **Tagline:** Vendor-neutral IT consulting for UAE organizations
- **Tone of Voice:** Professional, clear, outcome-focused
- **Avoid:** Technical jargon, sales pressure, feature lists without context
